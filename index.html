About Citesa

CITESA (Computer & IT Engineering Students' Association) of ISB&M School of Technology, Pune

Aims to play a pivotal role in the development of students as engineers as well as good human beings by conducting various extra-curricular activities

CITESA aims to inculcate an awareness about Social as well as technical culture of various disciplines.

It aims to build the personality of an individual as a good human being, and a perfect technician through its activities. The association provides a common platform for all the students of ISB&M .

CITESA provides a unique combination of enrichment activities, hands-on competitions, academic support, industry involvement and a supportive community environment

CITESA seeks to be an active organization of the Computer department at ISB&M School of Technology which promotes the students' career interests. The group continuously works for the overall development of the personality of the students in addition to get their academic responsibilities. CITESA gives wings to those planning to fly high and wanting to achieve excellence in their fields of interest
